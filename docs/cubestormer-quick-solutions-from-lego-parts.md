# CubeStormer 乐高零件快速解决方案

> 原文：<https://hackaday.com/2010/07/10/cubestormer-quick-solutions-from-lego-parts/>

[https://www.youtube.com/embed/eaRcWB3jwMo?version=3&rel=1&showsearch=0&showinfo=1&iv_load_policy=1&fs=1&hl=en-US&autohide=2&wmode=transparent](https://www.youtube.com/embed/eaRcWB3jwMo?version=3&rel=1&showsearch=0&showinfo=1&iv_load_policy=1&fs=1&hl=en-US&autohide=2&wmode=transparent)

CubeStormer 解魔方而且很快！完全由乐高制成的 Mindstorm 网络摄像机用于扫描立方体，四只机械手进行操作。该设备能够在不到 11 秒的时间内解决一个随机的立方体。这比我们上次看到的的[和](http://hackaday.com/2010/04/29/mindstorm-rubiks-solver/)[我们非常喜欢的立方体解算器](http://hackaday.com/2008/11/23/cubear-berkeleys-rubiks-cube-solver/)要快得多。
